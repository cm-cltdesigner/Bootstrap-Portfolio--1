About Courtney


She's a creative super woman with non-human super powers. She's originally from New Jersey who moved to Georgia to pursue her education Art & Design at the Art Institute of Atlanta. Later resided in Charlotte, NC with her husband and two daughters. She graduated from the Graphic Design Program at King's College. Charlotte is now home where she'll successfully and strategically design amazing solutions for your company. In 2019 she graduated with her Full Stack Web Development Certification from University of North Carolina at Charlotte.
